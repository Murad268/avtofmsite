What is Avto FM?

Avto FM is a radio network belonging to the Pro Media Group. The radio programming includes automative news and sports, as well as entertainment and music. Owned by Pro Media Group it has over 2000 broadcasters in all. It was founded in 13 October, 2013 and is broadcasted across the main cities of Azerbaijan.

Broadcast areas

Avto Fm broadcast area includes Absheron peninsula, Sumqait and the northern part of Azerbaijan.
